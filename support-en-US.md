## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. The app cannot connect to the backend. What should I do?

1. Confirm your backend is running (for example, `http://127.0.0.1:8000/health` is reachable).  
2. On a physical iPhone, do not use `127.0.0.1`; use your computer's LAN IP.  
3. Ensure phone and computer are on the same Wi-Fi network.  
4. Use "Auto Discover Local Server" in app settings.

### 2. Why is the leaderboard not updating after check-in?

1. Pull to refresh or tap refresh.  
2. Verify network connectivity.  
3. If the backend just started, wait a few seconds and try again.

### 3. Is the risk score a medical diagnosis?

No.  
The score is educational only and is not medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

### 4. Can I request data deletion?

Yes. Email support with details needed to locate records (for example timeframe and device context).

### 5. I forgot the server address. What should I do?

Use "Auto Discover Local Server" in Settings, or manually enter a reachable API URL.
