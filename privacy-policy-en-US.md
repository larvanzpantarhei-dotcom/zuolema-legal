# Zuolema Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-02-26  
Last updated: 2026-02-26

`Zuolema` ("the App", "we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use the App.

If you do not agree with this Policy, please stop using the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect:

1. Anonymous check-in and questionnaire data  
Includes check-ins, selected risk factors, and timestamps submitted in the App.  
We process this data in anonymized/de-identified form by default.

2. Regional and aggregated statistics  
Used to generate regional trend and leaderboard insights.

3. Device and log information  
Includes device model, OS version, crash logs, and request logs for reliability and security.

4. Information you provide directly  
If you contact support, we receive your email address and message content.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

1. Provide core features (anonymous check-ins, trend analytics, leaderboard display).  
2. Generate educational risk prompts and visual insights.  
3. Maintain service security, reliability, and abuse prevention.  
4. Respond to user support requests.  
5. Comply with applicable legal obligations.

## 3. Medical Disclaimer

The App provides educational and awareness content only.  
It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.  
Please consult a licensed healthcare professional for personal medical concerns.

## 4. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.  
We may disclose limited data only when:

1. You give explicit consent.  
2. Required by law, regulation, court order, or lawful government request.  
3. Needed to protect safety, rights, or prevent fraud/abuse.  
4. Shared with contracted service providers under confidentiality and security obligations.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.  
Afterward, data is deleted or anonymized.

## 6. Your Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights to:

1. Access or correct your information.  
2. Request deletion of data.  
3. Object to or restrict certain processing.  
4. Withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis.

You can submit requests using the contact details below.

## 7. Children

If we learn we collected child data inadvertently, we will delete it promptly.

## 9. International Transfers

Where permitted by law, data may be processed outside your country/region.
